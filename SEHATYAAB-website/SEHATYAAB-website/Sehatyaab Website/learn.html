ABOUT US

Sehatyaab, the first platform of Pakistan gives easy access to people to check whether the medicines are counterfeit, recalled, and/or expired with the help of a scanner. It also provides therapies and home remedies for people who don't prefer taking medicines; they can easily access the therapies or home remedies according to their suffering.

It is an online platform web along with a mobile-based application that allows health professionals and customers with a Smartphone to detect the fake medicines in order to have the surety of authentic and authorized medicines. It gives easy access to people to check whether the medicines are counterfeit, recalled, and/or expired with the help of a scanner. Additionally, provides an online portal for therapies and home remedies for people who don’t prefer using medicines; they can easily access the therapies or home remedies according to their suffering. Our application will also track the pharmacies that are available nearest to you so you can easily get your desired prescription, as well a list of all pharmacies that are originally certified by the government will also be generated so you may know which your go-to pharmacy is.
