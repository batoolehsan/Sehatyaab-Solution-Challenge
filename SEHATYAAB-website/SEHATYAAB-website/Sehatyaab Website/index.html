ABOUT US

As you all know that in Pakistan we've no platform where you could check the authenticity of the medicines. A platform where you would be sure that you're taking the right and an original prescription because these fake medicines may contain toxic doses of dangerous ingredients and cause mass poisoning. Sehatyaab core features are:

Detection of Fake Medicines

Allows you to detect the fake medicines by scanning them in order to have the surety of authentic and authorized medicines.

Nearest Pharmacy Location

Helps users to provide location and information of their nearest pharmacies.

Alerts for Medicine Schedule

Alert users to take the prescribed medicine as an alarm according to the schedule set by the user.
